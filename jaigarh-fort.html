Jaigarh Fort, Rajasthan

Click to View Glimpses

Explore the Royal Gallery
Tour Price: ₹3,200 / day

Location & History

Situated on the Cheel ka Teela (Hill of Eagles) in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Built in 1726 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II.

Description

Jaigarh Fort, also known as the Fort of Victory, houses the world's largest cannon on wheels - Jaivana Cannon. The fort offers stunning views of Amber Fort and Maota Lake.

Highlights

  • ✓Jaivana Cannon - world's largest wheeled cannon
  • ✓Water harvesting system of ancient times
  • ✓Laxmi Vilas palace with royal artifacts
  • ✓Underground passages connecting to Amber Fort
  • ✓Stunning hilltop views
