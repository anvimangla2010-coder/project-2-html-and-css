Mehrangarh Fort, Rajasthan

Click to View Glimpses

Explore the Royal Gallery
Tour Price: ₹4,000 / day

Location & History

Perched 400 feet above Jodhpur city in Rajasthan. Built around 1460 by Rao Jodha, it's one of the largest forts in India.

Description

Mehrangarh Fort stands magnificently on a 125-meter high hill, offering panoramic views of the blue city of Jodhpur. The fort houses several palaces with intricate carvings and expansive courtyards.
