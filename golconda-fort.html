Golconda Fort

Click to View Glimpses

Explore the Royal Gallery
Tour Price: ₹2,500 / day

Location & History

Located 11 km from Hyderabad, Telangana. Originally built in the 13th century, renovated by Qutub Shahi dynasty.

Description

Golconda Fort is famous for its acoustic system, diamond mines, and brilliant architecture. The fort was once home to the legendary Koh-i-Noor diamond.
