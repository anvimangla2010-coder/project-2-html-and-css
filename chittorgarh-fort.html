Location & History

Located in Chittorgarh, Rajasthan. One of the largest forts in India, covering 700 acres. Built in 7th century AD.

Description

Chittorgarh Fort is a symbol of Rajput pride and valor. The fort has witnessed three major sieges and houses numerous palaces, temples, and towers.
